## Requirements

### Requirement: Shared transport information

The transport section SHALL describe the buses available to all guests between the church and Villa Presto.

#### Scenario: Shared buses shown

- **WHEN** a visitor views the transport section
- **THEN** it states there is a bus from the church to Villa Presto after the ceremony
- **AND** it states there are return buses from Villa Presto

#### Scenario: Times may be pending

- **WHEN** exact bus times are not yet finalized
- **THEN** the section shows a clear placeholder rather than incorrect times
